About Us

Welcome to Hotel SunBird

Discover Hotel SunBird, your elegant haven in Sauraha, Nepal. Indulge in our luxurious, family-run establishment, which provides top-notch accommodations and mouthwatering Tharu cuisine. Enjoy incredible scenery of the peaceful Rapti River and Chitwan National Park in addition to exhilarating traditional performances. Modern comforts and true hospitality are added to your stay, including air-conditioned rooms, free Wi-Fi, with affordable costs. Quality and hospitality come together at Hotel SunBird to produce a memorable stay.
